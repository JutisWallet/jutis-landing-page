Send & Receive Tokens

send

Sending Tokens

  1. Open Jutis and select your wallet
  2. Click the "Send" button
  3. Enter recipient address
  4. Specify amount and confirm
receive

Receiving Tokens

  1. Click "Receive" in your wallet
  2. Copy your wallet address
  3. Share it with the sender
  4. Or scan the QR code
