Wallet Architecture

Non-Custodial Design

Jutis is a non-custodial wallet, meaning your private keys never leave your device. Only you have access to your funds.

  • check_circle Private keys encrypted locally
  • check_circle Seed phrase generated on device
  • check_circle No server holds your keys

Multi-Chain Support

Jutis supports the Canton network and connected blockchains, allowing you to manage all your assets in one place.
